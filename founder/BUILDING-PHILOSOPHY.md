# Building Philosophy

Treat Chronicle as a product that engineers will trust with their memory.

Trust is fragile.

A single hallucinated answer damages trust.

A single fake integration damages trust.

A single broken workflow damages trust.

---

Chronicle must feel:

Fast

Reliable

Evidence-backed

Predictable

---

When uncertain:

Stop.

Ask.

Do not invent.

---

The goal is not AI.

The goal is understanding.

---

The goal is not search.

The goal is reconstruction.

---

The goal is not dashboards.

The goal is clarity.
